About Parker

Parker is your ultimate parking assistant, designed to help you find parking spaces in popular locations like malls and event venues. Our goal is to provide real-time parking availability, fare details, and convenient search options based on location and vehicle type.

Our Mission

We aim to simplify the parking experience by providing accurate and up-to-date information about available parking slots, pricing, and other essential details for busy locations.

Our Vision

We envision a future where parking is no longer a hassle for people. By bridging the gap between drivers and available parking spaces, Parker aims to reduce parking stress and improve the overall driving experience.
